What is AIVScan?

AIVScan is a web-based security evaluation application that aims to efficiently and accurately assess the security of your computer. It is designed for all types of users, home or business, regardless of technical sophistication. Our service is an automatic security scan that determines Internet-accessible services, their vendor and version, and any known vulnerabilities that can endanger the user’s computer and stored information. AIVScan automatically obtains much of the information necessary to conduct the security scan, including IP address, operating system, and others.

The primary goal of AIVScan is to improve the overall security profile of our users’ computers. If our clients follow through with our suggestions for improvement, they will improve their computer’s security and help protect their sensitive information.
